# Customer Churn Prediction & Retention Analytics

## Overview

This project predicts customer churn for a telecom company using Machine Learning and identifies high-risk customers for targeted retention campaigns.

The system combines predictive analytics, business intelligence, and revenue-risk analysis to help organizations reduce customer attrition and improve retention strategies.

## Objectives

* Predict customer churn probability
* Segment customers into Low, Medium, and High Risk groups
* Identify customers requiring immediate retention action
* Estimate revenue at risk from potential churn
* Provide business recommendations through an interactive dashboard
